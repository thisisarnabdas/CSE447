Who We Are

Team Rainbow is a multinational task force comprised of the world's most highly trained and experienced counter-terrorism operators. Recruited from the ranks of elite special forces units, these Operators bring a unique blend of skills and expertise to the table, working together to neutralize the most dangerous threats on a global scale.

Our mission is simple: to protect civilian lives and prevent catastrophic events by any means necessary. We operate in the shadows, conducting high-risk operations in volatile regions to eliminate terrorist threats before they can come to fruition.
